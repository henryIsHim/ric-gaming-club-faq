The objective of the gaming club is to provide a place to share your gaming hobbies comfortably with each other, and also we aim to compete in esports tournaments if possible.

There are pretty much no requirements needed to join our club. If you are interested in playing games and want to share your interests with us, then you are welcome to join.

You can join the club by contacting our club president or any other members that you know. You can contact the club president via the social media links provided in the feedback page.
